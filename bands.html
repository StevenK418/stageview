Symbolic

Symbolic are a four piece progressive rock band based in Sheffield. The band was founded in 2015 and have been touring worldwide since the release of their EP, "Cymatics". The band features David Thompson on vocals, Jake teller on lead guitar, Danny Jefferson on bass and Tom Blackfield on drums. From humble beginnings in David's garage, the Band recorded their seminal EP with little else than a laptop. The song "Interference patterns" would prove to be a major hit with listeners nationwide and was the hit which skyrocketed the band to stardom. With their new latest release, "Onyx", the band is set to take the world by storm with this new wave of progressive rock.

Street Rats

Street rats are a four-person Punk band based in Bristol. Formed in 2017, the band took to the stage with their gritty, energy fuelled sound which is reminiscent of the genre's heyday on Britains streets. The band released it's first demo "Going Nowhere" in 2018 and have been a staple of the British rock scene ever since. With Jamie Anderson on vocals, Kyle Knox on guitar, Alex Teesdale on Bass and Daniel Johnson on drums, the bands raw energy and talent is best witnessed on stage with it's political themes, anger-fueled messaging and all round authenticity. The band are due to release their latest album "Swear Jar" later this year.

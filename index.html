Latest Band News

Symbolic, a four piece progressive rock band based in Sheffield, have just released their debut album, "Onyx". Rates 9/10 by NME, 10/10 by Kerrang radio and topping the rock charts, set to become Britain's #1 selling album of 2021. With the rise of this new British talent, we catch up with the band and see what plans lie ahead for these four denizens. We'll be talking everything from recording, touring to the tension between band members during their latest 2020 tour. Do not miss this exclusive interview with one of the greatest British bands of 2021.
